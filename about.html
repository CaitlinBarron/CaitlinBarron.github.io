I am a student at Rochester Institute of Technology, graduating in May 2020 with a BS degree in Computer Engineering.

At RIT I am heavily involved in the Baja SAE team, specifically the data aquisition group. The Baja team is a student run organization that designs and builds an off-road racing vehicle to compete in SAE sanctioned races across the country. The data aquisition team uses a wide variety of sensors and testing systems to help improve the design of the car through data. For example, we might set up hall-effect sensors to measure engine RPM and further improve the clutch gear ratio and efficiency based on those numbers.

I just recently completed an eight month software engineering internship at Saab Sensis in Syracuse NY. While there, I worked on software in C++ for air traffic control using mode C and mode S communications.
